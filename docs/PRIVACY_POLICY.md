# Privacy Policy

## Data Collection
Extension Privacy Guardian collects ZERO personal data.

## Local Processing
All extension scans are performed locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers.

## Storage
Scan results are stored in your browser's IndexedDB. Data never leaves your device.

## Permissions
We request only these permissions:
- management: To read installed extension metadata
- storage: To save scan results locally

## Contact
Questions? Open an issue on GitHub: https://github.com/CSroseX/SafeExtensions/issues
